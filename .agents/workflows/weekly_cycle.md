# Weekly Collaboration Cycle

## 1. Monday: Planning & Research
- **No technical work (coding) is permitted.**
- Perform deep research on the target feature or module.
- Define goals and provide detailed walkthroughs to the user.
- Iterate until the user provides full approval of the goals.

## 2. Tuesday Morning: Action Plan Finalization
- Convert approved goals into a step-by-step action plan.
- Lock the plan. 
- **Once locked (by Tuesday noon), no further changes or additions to the scope are allowed for the rest of the week.**

## 3. Wed-Fri: Execution
- Implement the locked plan.
- Verify and demonstrate results.
